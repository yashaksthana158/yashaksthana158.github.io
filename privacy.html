RAG Chat

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2025
RAG Chat is an academic research project. We collect only what is necessary to operate the service and never sell or share your data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — your email address when you sign up or sign in via Google / GitHub OAuth.
  • Conversation data — messages you send and receive, stored to enable persistent chat history.
  • Uploaded documents — PDFs or URLs you provide for document-based Q&A, processed as vector embeddings.
  • Session data — authentication tokens managed by Supabase to keep you signed in.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To store and display your conversation history across sessions.
  • To process documents you upload for retrieval-augmented question answering.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the RAG Chat service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account and conversation data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database protected by Row Level Security (RLS), encrypted connections (TLS), and OAuth token management. We never store your Google or GitHub password.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and storage.
  • NVIDIA AI Endpoints / Groq — language model inference.
  • DuckDuckGo Search — optional web search (queries are not linked to your identity).
  • Google / GitHub OAuth — optional sign-in providers.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. Conversations older than 30 days may be automatically deleted. You can delete individual conversations at any time. Guest sessions are stored only in your browser's memory and lost when you close the tab.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, deletion of, or export of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Cookies

RAG Chat uses only session cookies required for authentication. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us: csresearch@andc.du.ac.in
